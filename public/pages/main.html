Welcome to BCIT Computing Club

Connect, Build, Engage

Info

Connect

BCIT's computing club prioritizes the success of other BCIT students in pursuring a tech related career and a general interest in technology. Networking events and hackathons are hosted to improve student bonds and create strong connections between students before going into the workforce.BCIT is an all inclusive club that supports students who have an interest in tech no matter their background. Strong empahsis is put on supporting marginal groups within the tech industry and demonstrating our pride in having equal opportunities for students.

Build

Hack The Break is BCIT's biggest hackathon with over 100 participants last year and over 2500 dollars in cash prizes. Hack The Breaks main goal is to introduce students to the competiveness of hackathons and the numerous benefits they provide.

Engage

A community within the tech community is something that BCIT computing Club aims to build within the school.
